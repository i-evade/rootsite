Online Safety Tips

Stay safe online by following these tips:

  • Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts.
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information online.
  • Regularly update your software and antivirus programs.
  • Use secure and reputable websites for online transactions.
  • Enable two-factor authentication when possible.

Cyberbullying Awareness

Learn about cyberbullying and how to prevent it:

  • Be aware of online behaviors that may constitute cyberbullying.
  • Report and block individuals engaging in cyberbullying.
  • Encourage open communication with friends and family about online experiences.
  • Teach others about the impact of cyberbullying on mental health.

Grooming Prevention

Protect yourself from online grooming with these tips:

  • Be cautious when sharing personal information with strangers online.
  • Keep your social media profiles private and carefully manage friend requests.
  • Educate yourself and others about the tactics used by online groomers.
  • Report suspicious behavior to the appropriate authorities.
